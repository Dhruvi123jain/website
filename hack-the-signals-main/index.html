HACK THE SIGNALS

Get ready for Hack The Signals, the ultimate 24-hour AI Signal Processing hackathon brought to you by IEEE Computer Society and Signal Processing Society College of Engineering. We challenge you to tackle innovative and sustainable solutions for cutting-edge AI and Signal Processing hurdles. Hack The Signals is more than just a 2-day event — it’s a complete experience. Here you can dive into brainstorming sessions, panel discussions, and hands-on expert sessions, all in one place. Don’t miss this chance to network with industry leaders, AI pioneers, and fellow enthusiasts in an exciting and high-energy environment. Meet new friends and mentors, make great connections, and learn new skills and perspectives. No matter if you’ve never tried solving problems before, you’ll step out transformed and inspired. Join us at Hack The Signals and be part of something truly transformative.

Guidelines for the Hackathon

  • Be at the Registration Desk by 9 AM sharp.
  • Bring a valid ID card.
  • Bring a valid copy of your IEEE, Computer Society, and Signal Processing Society membership - provide your membership card depending on membership category.
  • Each team must consist of 2 to 4 members.
  • Every team must have a unique team name.
  • A cloakroom will be provided inside the venue; however, the organizers won’t be held responsible for any lost wallets, ornaments, etc.
  • Mentoring sessions will take place on August 31st.
  • The hackathon kicks off at 9:00 PM on August 31st.
  • The Problem Statement will be revealed before the hackathon begins.
  • Teams must stop coding when the time is up but can debug and make minor fixes. Adding new features or making significant changes after that is not permitted.
  • Fresh-up rooms will be available on the second day.
  • No refunds will be processed after payment.
  • Don’t leave the premises without valid consent from event coordinators.
